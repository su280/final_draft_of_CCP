My brother learned to make these cookies at a baking camp which he then brought this delicious recipe home. He makes it for us once every week where we eat with warm milk.

Recipe Ingredients

Unsalted butter, Granulated Sugar, Packed light or dark brown sugar, Large egg, Pure vanilla extract, Semi-sweet chocolate chunks (melted), All-purpose flour, Natural unsweetened cocoa powder, Baking soda, Salt, Semi-sweet chocolate chunks
